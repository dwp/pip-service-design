Apply for Personal Independence Payment (PIP) How your condition affects your daily life

The next questions are about how your health condition affects you in your daily life. Your responses will help us determine your entitlement to PIP.

We'll ask you about:

  • travelling from place to place
  • shopping for essential items
  • doing the housework
  • going to medical appointments
  • going to work
  • preparing meals
  • budgeting

We know talking about your health condition may be difficult, but we need you to tell us as much as you can. This will help us make the right decision on your claim.
